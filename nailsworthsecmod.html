Nailsworth Secondary Modern School

Updated 11 July 2009

Here are some nostalgic photos of Nailsworth School in the 1950s and 1960s, the group shots courtesy of former Highwoodian 'Ann'.

  • Nailsworth Secondary Modern School was opened in the old British school building in 1951. 

  • By 1959, the School had 200 pupils. 

  • In 1964, the School moved to new buildings on the forest Green campus and became known as Highwood School.

 
 

School Cooks: Ms Hooper, Stanton and Beard with Headmaster Mr Wallis in the 1950s
